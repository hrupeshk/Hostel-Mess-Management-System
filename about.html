About Patkai Hostel Mess

Welcome to the official website of Patkai Hostel Mess at Tezpur University! Our hostel is dedicated to providing a comfortable and conducive living environment for our students, ensuring their well-being and academic success.

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a home away from home for our students. We strive to offer a safe, inclusive, and nurturing environment that promotes personal growth, academic excellence, and community spirit.

About the Mess

The Patkai Men's Hostel Mess is an integral part of our community, providing nutritious and delicious meals to our residents. Our mess team is committed to maintaining high standards of hygiene and quality in food preparation, ensuring that students have access to balanced meals throughout the week.

Mess Management System

Our website serves as a comprehensive platform for managing the hostel mess. Features include:

  • Weekly Menu: Students can view the weekly menu to plan their meals.
  • Fee Status: Students can check their mess fee payment status.
  • Feedback: Students can provide feedback to help us improve our services.
  • Edit Profile: Students can update their personal information as needed.

Community and Activities

At Patkai Men's Hostel, we believe in fostering a strong sense of community. We organize various activities and events throughout the year to encourage interaction and camaraderie among students. From cultural nights to sports tournaments, there's always something happening at Patkai!
